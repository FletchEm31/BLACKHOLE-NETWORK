SECTION 1 — ARBITRAGE_POSITIONS (full trade lifecycle)
================================================================================
Tracks every arbitrage trade from signal detection through settlement.
Each row represents one complete trade: buy on Market A, sell on Market B.

CARD IDENTITY:
  id                    SERIAL PRIMARY KEY
  card_id               INTEGER REFERENCES master_card_catalog(id)
  card_code             TEXT              -- BST-004-1E
  slab_code             TEXT              -- BST-004-1E-PSA-10
  card_name             TEXT
  set_name              TEXT
  grader                TEXT
  grade                 TEXT

SIGNAL LINKAGE (what triggered the trade):
  signal_id             INTEGER REFERENCES tokenized_arbitrage_signals(id)
  signal_strength       TEXT              -- weak / moderate / strong / critical
  signal_spread_pct     DECIMAL(6,2)      -- gross spread at signal time
  signal_detected_at    TIMESTAMPTZ

POSITION STATUS:
  status                TEXT NOT NULL CHECK (status IN (
                          'signal_detected',  -- signal fired, awaiting operator decision
                          'buy_pending',      -- committed to buy, transaction not confirmed
                          'bought',           -- purchase confirmed, holding position
                          'relist_pending',   -- creating sell listing
                          'listed',           -- actively listed for sale on target market
                          'sale_pending',     -- buyer committed, awaiting settlement
                          'sold',             -- sale completed, position closed
                          'redeemed',         -- token redeemed for physical card
                          'shipped',          -- physical card shipped to buyer
                          'cancelled',        -- trade abandoned (no loss if pre-buy)
                          'expired'           -- listing expired without sale
                        ))
  status_updated_at     TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()

DIRECTION:
  direction             TEXT NOT NULL CHECK (direction IN (
                          'courtyard_to_courtyard',   -- buy token, relist same platform
                          'courtyard_to_ebay',        -- buy token, redeem physical, sell eBay
                          'courtyard_to_cc',           -- buy Courtyard, sell Collector Crypt
                          'ebay_to_courtyard',         -- buy physical, vault + tokenize, sell
                          'ebay_to_cc',                -- buy physical, vault on CC, sell
                          'cc_to_courtyard',           -- buy CC token, sell on Courtyard
                          'cc_to_ebay',                -- buy CC token, redeem, sell eBay
                          'cc_to_cc',                  -- relist within Collector Crypt
                          'within_opensea'             -- buy/sell same token on OpenSea
                        ))

BUY SIDE (acquisition):
  buy_market            TEXT              -- 'courtyard', 'collector_crypt', 'ebay'
  buy_item_id           TEXT              -- token ID or eBay item ID
  buy_price             DECIMAL(10,2)     -- what you paid
  bought_at             TIMESTAMPTZ
  buy_tx_hash           TEXT              -- blockchain tx hash (tokenized only)

BUY-SIDE COSTS (actual):
  buy_platform_fee      DECIMAL(10,2)
  buy_processing_fee    DECIMAL(10,2)
  buy_gas               DECIMAL(10,6)
  buy_total_cost        DECIMAL(10,2)     -- buy_price + all buy-side fees

SELL SIDE (disposition):
  sell_market            TEXT             -- 'courtyard', 'collector_crypt', 'ebay'
  sell_item_id           TEXT
  sell_listed_price      DECIMAL(10,2)   -- what you listed at
  sell_final_price       DECIMAL(10,2)   -- what it actually sold for
  listed_at              TIMESTAMPTZ
  sold_at                TIMESTAMPTZ
  sell_tx_hash           TEXT

SELL-SIDE COSTS (actual):
  sell_platform_fee      DECIMAL(10,2)
  sell_processing_fee    DECIMAL(10,2)
  sell_royalty_fee       DECIMAL(10,2)
  sell_auth_fee          DECIMAL(10,2)    -- eBay AG
  sell_shipping          DECIMAL(10,2)
  sell_gas               DECIMAL(10,6)
  sell_total_deductions  DECIMAL(10,2)   -- sum of all sell-side costs
  sell_net_proceeds      DECIMAL(10,2)   -- sell_final_price - sell_total_deductions

REDEMPTION / PHYSICAL MOVEMENT (Direction B/C only):
  redemption_requested_at TIMESTAMPTZ
  redemption_completed_at TIMESTAMPTZ
  redemption_fee          DECIMAL(10,2)
  shipping_tracking       TEXT
  shipping_cost           DECIMAL(10,2)
  shipping_carrier        TEXT

VAULT / TOKENIZATION (physical → token, Direction C only):
  vault_submitted_at      TIMESTAMPTZ
  vault_authenticated_at  TIMESTAMPTZ
  token_minted_at         TIMESTAMPTZ
  tokenization_fee        DECIMAL(10,2)

SECTION 2 — THREE-WAY COST COMPARISON (market vs estimated vs actual)
================================================================================
For each cost line item, track three values:
  MARKET    = published platform rate (from fee_schedule)
  ESTIMATED = pre-trade projection (from estimate_trade_costs() at signal time)
  ACTUAL    = what was actually charged (populated after trade closes)

PURPOSE:
  Market vs Estimated → shows promo savings or special pricing
  Estimated vs Actual → measures estimate accuracy (calibration data)
  Market vs Actual    → total savings vs published rates

COLUMNS ON arbitrage_positions:

PLATFORM FEES:
  market_platform_fee       DECIMAL(10,2)   -- published rate for this trade
  est_platform_fee          DECIMAL(10,2)   -- estimated at signal time
  actual_platform_fee       DECIMAL(10,2)   -- actually charged

PAYMENT PROCESSING:
  market_processing_fee     DECIMAL(10,2)
  est_processing_fee        DECIMAL(10,2)
  actual_processing_fee     DECIMAL(10,2)

SHIPPING & HANDLING:
  market_shipping           DECIMAL(10,2)   -- typical/average shipping cost
  est_shipping              DECIMAL(10,2)   -- estimated at signal time
  actual_shipping           DECIMAL(10,2)   -- actual shipping paid

AUTHENTICATION:
  market_auth_fee           DECIMAL(10,2)   -- eBay AG published midpoint
  est_auth_fee              DECIMAL(10,2)
  actual_auth_fee           DECIMAL(10,2)

REDEMPTION / VAULT:
  market_redemption_fee     DECIMAL(10,2)
  est_redemption_fee        DECIMAL(10,2)
  actual_redemption_fee     DECIMAL(10,2)

GAS:
  market_gas_fee            DECIMAL(10,2)
  est_gas_fee               DECIMAL(10,2)
  actual_gas_fee            DECIMAL(10,2)

ROLLUP TOTALS:
  market_total_costs        DECIMAL(10,2)   -- sum of all market rates
  est_total_costs           DECIMAL(10,2)   -- sum of all estimates
  actual_total_costs        DECIMAL(10,2)   -- sum of all actuals

DELTAS:
  delta_market_vs_est       DECIMAL(10,2)   -- market - estimated (promo/special savings)
  delta_est_vs_actual       DECIMAL(10,2)   -- actual - estimated (estimate accuracy)
  delta_market_vs_actual    DECIMAL(10,2)   -- market - actual (total savings vs list rate)

PROFIT (three views):
  market_net_profit         DECIMAL(10,2)   -- profit if full published fees were paid
  est_net_profit            DECIMAL(10,2)   -- projected profit at signal time
  actual_net_profit         DECIMAL(10,2)   -- real profit (THE number that matters)

RETURN METRICS:
  est_roi_pct               DECIMAL(6,2)    -- estimated ROI at signal time
  actual_roi_pct            DECIMAL(6,2)    -- actual ROI after trade closes
  roi_variance_pct          DECIMAL(6,2)    -- actual - estimated (how far off?)
  days_held                 INT             -- sold_at - bought_at
  annualized_return_pct     DECIMAL(8,2)    -- (actual_roi_pct / days_held) * 365


================================================================================
SECTION 3 — RISK MANAGEMENT FIELDS
================================================================================

  max_loss_threshold      DECIMAL(10,2)   -- stop-loss: sell if unrealized loss hits this
  target_profit           DECIMAL(10,2)   -- take-profit: target at time of entry
  price_at_risk           DECIMAL(10,2)   -- current market price vs buy (HORIZON updates)
  unrealized_pnl          DECIMAL(10,2)   -- current market - buy_total_cost (HORIZON updates)

NOTES:
  price_at_risk and unrealized_pnl are updated by HORIZON periodically
  (e.g. every hour for open positions) by checking current asks on the
  sell-side market. This enables real-time position monitoring:

  HORIZON SMS alert:
    "RISK: BST-004-1E-PSA-10 — bought $800, current market $720.
     Unrealized loss: -$80 (-10%). Stop-loss threshold: -$100.
     Action needed within 24h or auto-delist."

METADATA:
  notes                   TEXT
  created_at              TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()
  updated_at              TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()

INDEXES:
  arb_pos_card_id_idx     ON (card_id)
  arb_pos_status_idx      ON (status)
  arb_pos_direction_idx   ON (direction)
  arb_pos_open_idx        ON (status) WHERE status IN ('bought','listed','relist_pending')

SECTION 4 — ESTIMATE ACCURACY TRACKING (CALIBRATION)
================================================================================
Over time, the three-way comparison builds a dataset of how accurate the
estimates are. This is used to improve the fee_schedule and the estimation
function. Run periodically (weekly) as a calibration check.

KEY QUERY — How accurate are cost estimates by direction?

  SELECT
    direction,
    COUNT(*) AS trades_completed,
    AVG(delta_est_vs_actual) AS avg_cost_miss,
    AVG(ABS(delta_est_vs_actual)) AS avg_absolute_miss,
    STDDEV(delta_est_vs_actual) AS cost_miss_stddev,
    AVG(roi_variance_pct) AS avg_roi_miss,
    MAX(ABS(delta_est_vs_actual)) AS worst_cost_miss,
    AVG(actual_total_costs) AS avg_actual_costs,
    AVG(est_total_costs) AS avg_estimated_costs
  FROM arbitrage_positions
  WHERE status = 'sold'
    AND actual_total_costs IS NOT NULL
  GROUP BY direction
  ORDER BY AVG(ABS(delta_est_vs_actual)) DESC;

INTERPRETATION:
  avg_cost_miss > 0  → estimates are too LOW (underestimating costs)
                        → update fee_schedule rates upward
  avg_cost_miss < 0  → estimates are too HIGH (overestimating costs)
                        → could be capturing promos, update fee_schedule
  avg_absolute_miss > $20 → estimates are unreliable, investigate
  cost_miss_stddev high → costs are unpredictable (likely shipping variance)

ACTIONS:
  If courtyard_to_ebay consistently misses by +$15:
    → shipping_flat estimate ($8) is too low
    → UPDATE fee_schedule SET rate = 12.00
      WHERE market = 'ebay' AND fee_name = 'Shipping (graded card)';
    → Future estimates automatically improve

KEY QUERY — What promos actually saved you vs published rates?

  SELECT
    direction,
    SUM(delta_market_vs_actual) AS total_promo_savings,
    AVG(delta_market_vs_actual) AS avg_savings_per_trade,
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE delta_market_vs_actual > 0) AS trades_with_savings
  FROM arbitrage_positions
  WHERE status = 'sold'
  GROUP BY direction;


================================================================================
SECTION 5 — HORIZON P&L REPORTING
================================================================================
HORIZON queries arbitrage_positions to generate daily/weekly summaries.

DAILY SUMMARY QUERY:

  SELECT
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'sold'
      AND sold_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '24 hours') AS trades_closed_today,
    SUM(actual_net_profit) FILTER (WHERE status = 'sold'
      AND sold_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '24 hours') AS net_profit_today,
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status IN ('bought','listed')) AS open_positions,
    SUM(buy_total_cost) FILTER (WHERE status IN ('bought','listed')) AS capital_deployed,
    SUM(unrealized_pnl) FILTER (WHERE status IN ('bought','listed')) AS total_unrealized,
    AVG(actual_roi_pct) FILTER (WHERE status = 'sold'
      AND sold_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '30 days') AS avg_roi_30d,
    AVG(days_held) FILTER (WHERE status = 'sold'
      AND sold_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '30 days') AS avg_hold_days_30d
  FROM arbitrage_positions;

HORIZON SMS FORMAT:

  "DAILY P&L | May 27, 2026
   Closed: 3 trades | Net: +$847.25
   Avg ROI: 22.4% | Avg hold: 2.3 days
   Open: 5 positions | Deployed: $3,200
   Unrealized: +$412 across open positions
   Best: BST-004-1E-PSA-10 +$336 (28.1%)
   Worst: TRK-004-UN-CGC-9 -$12 (-1.8%)"

WEEKLY SUMMARY ADDS:
  - Estimate accuracy: "Cost estimates off by avg $8.50 (2.1%)"
  - Direction performance: "courtyard_to_courtyard: 12 trades, avg +$45"
  - Fee savings: "Saved $132 vs published eBay rates (promo active)"
